Global Context

Global Perspectives & Youth Representation

The conference brings together diverse voices to examine youth democratic participation across different political systems and cultural contexts. We focus on understanding both challenges and opportunities for meaningful youth engagement in governance.

  • Comparative analysis of youth political participation across democracies
  • Innovative models of youth representation in governance
  • Digital democracy and youth civic engagement
  • Intergenerational dialogue and policy transfer
